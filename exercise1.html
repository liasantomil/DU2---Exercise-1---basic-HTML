Dolphins have become well known for their sociable nature and high intelligence.

Characteristics

  • Physical Appearance: Dolphins range in color from light gray to dark gray on their backs, which lightens to white on their underbellies and beneath their jaws

  • Social Behavior: Dolphins form strong bonds with other dolphins, and if one dolphin become injured, the others help it to reach the surface.
  • Playful Nature
  • Language and Intelligence: Dolphins are known for their intelligence and ability to communicate with each other.
